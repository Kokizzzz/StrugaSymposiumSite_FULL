Enesa Memeti

North Macedonia • Art Teacher & Curator

🎨 About

Professor Enesa Memeti teaches visual arts at the Gjoce Stojčeski Vocational High School in Tetovo, where she organizes and curates student exhibitions focused on thematic and professional development.

💼 Teaching & Exhibitions

Her work includes multiple student-led exhibitions: – “7 March” at the Culture Palace (2012 & 2017), – “Golden Years” with graduating classes (2016 & 2021, Gallery of Arts Tetovo), – “Diary of Knowledge” (2021) :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}. In April 2025, she opened her second solo show titled “The Black Swan: Symbol of Magnificence” at KIC Skopje, following participation in around 30 group exhibitions :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.

🖼️ Curation & Stylistic Themes

Enesa’s curatorial practice highlights student creativity through thematic exhibitions emphasizing symbolism, cultural narratives, and professional presentation. Her solo shows explore metaphor‑rich subjects—such as the Black Swan—to convey elegance and emotional depth.

🤝 Role at Struga ’25

At Struga ’25, Enesa brings her strong background in art education and exhibition-making. She will curate collaborative pupil shows, lead thematic workshops, and mentor emerging artists in thematic storytelling and exhibition curation.
