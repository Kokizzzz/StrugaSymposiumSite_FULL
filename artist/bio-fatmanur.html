Fatmanur Savruk

Turkey • Painter & Printmaker

🎨 About

Fatmanur Savruk was born in Edirne in 1997. She graduated from Edirne Hasan Rıza Fine Arts High School in 2015 and completed her BA in Art Teaching at Trakya University (2015–2019). She began her MA in Art Teaching at the same university in 2019 :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.

💼 Work

From 2018 to 2019, Fatmanur taught painting at Edirne‑Meriç Public Education Center and has since participated in numerous group exhibitions both in Turkey and abroad :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}. She was featured in the "Kesişen Yollar" group show on World Art Day through ArtsOnline Gallery :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.

🖌️ Style & Themes

Fatmanur's work is characterized by expressive portraiture and original printmaking techniques, often integrating natural and allegorical motifs—demonstrating both technical skill and thematic depth.

🤝 Role at Struga ’25

As a young educator and practicing artist, Fatmanur will lead portrait and printmaking workshops that focus on translating emotion into visual language and guiding participants through layered technical processes.
