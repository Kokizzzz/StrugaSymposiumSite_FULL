Jëlldëz Asani

North Macedonia • Art Historian & Teaching Assistant

🎓 Credentials

Jëlldëz Asani holds a PhD (2017) from the International University of Novi Pazar with a thesis on Albanian contemporary painting. He earned an MA (2010) and BA (2002) from Ss. Cyril and Methodius University, Skopje, focusing on Albanian painters and Impressionism in the region :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.

🏛️ Academic Role

Since 2013, he has been a Teaching Assistant in the Department of Arts at the University of Tetova’s Faculty of Arts. He also worked as an assistant from 2007–2013, and previously served as an archaeologist at the National Conservation Center in Skopje :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.

📚 Publications

On April 23, 2025, the University of Tetova celebrated his two latest scholarly books: *From Renaissance to the Optical Illusion of Mannerism* and *Light‑Shadows of Baroque & Theatricality of Rococo*. These works contribute significantly to European art studies in Albanian :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.

🖌️ Expertise & Impact

His expertise spans Western figurative art, with a chronological and analytical approach that appeals to students, researchers, and art enthusiasts exploring stylistic developments from the Renaissance to Rococo :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}.

🤝 Role at Struga ’25

At Struga ’25, Jëlldëz contributes his deep knowledge of Western art history through workshops and lectures focused on Renaissance–Rococo periods, enriching the intellectual depth of the symposium.
