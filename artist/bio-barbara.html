Barbara Demšar

Slovenia • Painter & Collage Artist

🎨 About

Born in 1969 in Kranj, Barbara graduated in painting from the University of Ljubljana’s Faculty of Education in 1997, studying under Prof. Herman Gvardjančič. Since then, she has been a freelance artist based in Škofja Loka and a member of key art associations in Slovenia :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.

💼 Work & Exhibitions

Barbara’s practice blends acrylic, collage, and abstract motifs inspired by light, nature, and dreamlike landscapes. Her cycle “Obsijani s soncem” (2021) was showcased in a virtual exhibition at the Loški Muzej :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.

🏛️ Collections & Style

Her works are represented by Galerija Mak in Ljubljana and featured internationally via Agora Gallery and Saatchi Art — notable for their luminous, symbolic abstraction :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}.

🤝 Role at Struga ’25

Barbara brings her lyrical, symbol-rich approach to workshops focused on abstraction and collage techniques, fostering creative dialogue on the interplay of light, material, and narrative.
