Drenusha Palloshi

North Macedonia • Assistant Professor & Art Researcher

🎓 Academic Profile

Dr. Drenusha Palloshi is an Assistant Professor in the Faculty of Arts at the University of Tetovo. She earned her BA (2008–2012) and MA (2014–2015) with theses exploring music–painting synesthesia—“Sounds of Chords in Paintings” and “Musical Reminiscences in Paintings” respectively :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.

🏛️ Teaching & Research

Since December 2018, she has served as an Assistant Professor at UT’s Faculty of Arts, teaching courses in painting, art theory, and interdisciplinary art research. She is fluent in Albanian, Macedonian, English, Serbian, and Spanish :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.

💼 Community Engagement

Drenusha has contributed artworks to public benefit exhibitions—like a collective donation of paintings for social causes organized by local cultural institutions :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.

🤝 Role at Struga ’25

With her background in interdisciplinary art research and teaching, Drenusha will lead theoretical and practical workshops exploring the dialogue between visual art and music, as well as mentoring emerging artists.
