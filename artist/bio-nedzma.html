Nedžma Zukorlić

Serbia – Painter & Emerging Talent

🎨 About

Born April 9, 1996 in Novi Pazar, Nedžma studied painting at the Gazi Isa-beg Madrasa. She earned her BA in Painting (2019) and later completed her Master’s at the Faculty of Fine Arts in Belgrade :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.

💼 Exhibitions & Awards

She has held two solo exhibitions in Novi Pazar and Belgrade and participated in over 36 group exhibitions and three international art colonies :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}. She won third prize at the Youth Art Salon in Novi Pazar :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.

In 2023, she co-exhibited with Nevena Milosavljević in “Ispod površine” at Gallery ’73 in Belgrade, followed by the “Senzibilitet u formi” show in Sarajevo :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}.

🎨 Style & Themes

Her work explores introspection and subtle sensibilities, both in form and content—often centering on personal and cultural identity through expressive portraiture and layered compositions.

🤝 Role at Struga ’25

At Struga ’25, Nedžma brings youthful perspective and teaching experience. She will lead painting workshops focused on emotional expression, portraiture, and narrative visual language.
