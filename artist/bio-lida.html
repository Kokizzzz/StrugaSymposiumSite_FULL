Lida Sherafatmand

🎨 About

Lida Sherafatmand (born Iran) is a painter, writer, and humanitarian artist. She founded and promotes “Florescencism”—an avant-garde movement rooted in beauty, healing, and connection, challenging trauma-focused art narratives :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.

💼 Exhibitions & Collections

She has exhibited globally—solo and group shows in Venice (Biennale 2019), Tokyo, Geneva, Malta (European Parliament 2024), Netherlands, New Zealand, Struga International Poetry Festival 2023, and more :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.

🌍 Social Impact

Lida volunteers across Education, Health, Peace Building, and Cultural Development. Her paintings are used in hospitals, diplomatic settings, and interfaith gatherings, emphasising healing and unity :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}.

🖌️ Artistic Philosophy

Rooted in neurosaesthetics, energy medicine, and poetry, Florescencism emphasizes emotional restoration. Her works have been adapted as stamps (Liechtenstein, China), book covers, and institutional installations :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}.

🤝 Role at Struga ’25

At Struga ’25, Lida will offer workshops and dialogues centered on Florescencism, emotional wellness through art, and interfaith peace-building projects inspired by her global cultural practice.
