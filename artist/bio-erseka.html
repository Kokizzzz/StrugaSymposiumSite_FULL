Erseka Mulliqi

Kosovo • Emerging Artist

🎨 About

Erseka Mulliqi is a visual arts student based in Kosovo, currently pursuing her studies at the University of Prishtina “Hasan Prishtina”. She is part of a new generation of emerging artists engaging in national cultural projects and regional creative events.

💼 Work

While still early in her career, Erseka has participated in state-sponsored artistic colonies and public exhibitions that support rising talents from Kosovo’s academic institutions. Her focus includes contemporary media and community-centered themes.

🤝 Role at Struga ’25

As one of the youngest participants at Struga ’25, Erseka contributes fresh perspectives and experimental approaches, participating in collaborative workshops and creative exchange across cultures.
